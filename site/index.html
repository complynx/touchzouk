About Me

Hi, I’m Daniel Drizhuk — DJ Touchzouk. I craft emotive Brazilian Zouk journeys where subtle storytelling meets precision groove. Based in Amsterdam, I blend modern Zouk, NeoZouk, R&B, silky pop edits, and tasteful remixes into long-form arcs that breathe on the dance floor.

I started in 2010 as a song selector for local dance communities and, in 2016, completed DJing and music-production courses with DJ Kakah and DJ Brixx. Since then I’ve played Zouk events across Brazil, Mexico, and Europe.

From 2018 to 2022 I hosted weekly parties in Moscow. Since 2019 I’ve been part of the Zouk Non Stop Marathon core team, and during the pandemic I was one of the main DJs for IHZR. Since 2023 I’ve been based in Amsterdam, performing at both local and international events.
